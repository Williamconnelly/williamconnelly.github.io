MovieList

LIVE SITE CODE

With MovieList, I got to combine my love of coding with my extreme passion for visual media and cataloguing. MovieList is a movie indexing application which allows users to search for their favorite movies or movies they’ve seen, add them to a list, rate them, and sort through their collection in a variety of ways.

While there are a variety of other movie cataloguing sites out there, they place hardly any emphasis on user ratings and user information. With MovieList, the user’s average rating is displayed on their profile page and they can compare their own individual movie ratings against the aggregated opinions of sites such as IMDB, Metacritic, and Rotten Tomatoes.

My long term goal for the project would be to eventually pivot away from the current OMDB API in favor of a more consistent and detailed movie database in order to better display movie details and extrapolate engaging viewer information.
